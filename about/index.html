ABOUT

I am a fourth-year undergraduate student at the University of Houston, studying both math and music. In my music, I try to innovate using detailed oriented processes, sometimes involving mathematics. I study composition with Rob Smith and I studied viola with Suzanne LeFevre.

I use a variety of programs for my work: MuseScore for music engraving, Inkscape for graphic design, and LaTeX for engraving math. On the programming side of things, I use Python and MATLAB for numerical computation and app creation. Recently, I've been getting to learn HTML/CSS for web development and SQL for database management.
